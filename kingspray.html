Kingspray VR Modding

Development of different maps for Kingspray Graffiti VR

Kingspray VR is a street art and graffiti simulator in virtual reality, which allows you to create your own maps in Unity and paint them in user-created environments.


I create maps for the simulator all the time and usually base the development of the environments on real places. All 3D objects in the maps shown here are modeled by me and optimized for use in VR.

Mine and other people's maps can be found on the Kingspray Leveldesign Discord server (https://discord.gg/awsjd6Q5A2 ).
