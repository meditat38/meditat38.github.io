everypaint

A VR-Multiplayer-App to paint on the Oculus Quest

"Everypaint" is a VR-Creative-Application that I developed as part of my bachelor thesis. Here, users can create their own environments from given objects in a collage-like manner and then paint on them.


The application is intended to unite the different types of VR-Creative-Applications, here three-dimensional art can be created, like in TiltBrush by Google or ShapesXR, and then painted in two dimensions as in painting simulators, like in Kingspray VR or Painting VR. The application is thus different from the simulators and is intended to be more of a tool for creating virtual art.

There are various tools available for painting the created environment, such as the marker and the spray can, which are more of a simulation of real painting tools. With the paintgun, however, there is also a tool that was specially developed for use in Virtual Reality and doesn't have much to do with reality. In addition, the multiplayer mode allows creating virtual art together.

"Everypaint" is released for the Oculus Quest on SideQuest (https://sidequestvr.com/app/9537/everypaint) free to use.
